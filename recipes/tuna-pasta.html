Tuna Pasta

Home

Delicious pasta dish with canned tuna and fresh ingredients

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna in Oil
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Dill
  • Boiled Eggs
  • Almonds
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Pasta

Method

  1. Cook Pasta according to packet
  2. Add boiled eggs, tomatoes, herbs, spices and avocado to pasta
  3. Lightly toast the almonds in oven, top pasta mix with them
